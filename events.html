Hanami Festival

(花見)

Hanami Festival is celebrating the traditional Japanese custom of enjoying flowers. This festival coincides with the blooming forecast. outdoor parties will occur underneath the vibrantly pink cherry blossom trees, where people will gather together and enjoy food, drinks, and celebrations

When

Early-March

Location

Sumida Park

Child-friendly

Yes

Kamakura Festival

(鎌倉まつり)

Kamakura Festival is s spring festival to celebrate Kamakura and the samurai spirit with traditional performances and daring feats of archery. The highlight of the festival is traditional performances such as the "Shizuno-Mai" dance, and yabusame horseback archery

When

Mid-April

Location

Tsurugaoka Hachimangu

Child-friendly

Yes

Natsumatsuri

(夏祭り)

Natsumatsuri is a collective term for festivals observed in summer. It tends to have originated in the events observed in rural communities to relieve farmers' fatigue from their agricultural work. It is now observed as a glamorous event rather than a solemn ceremony

When

Early-July

Location

Ueno Park

Child-friendly

Yes

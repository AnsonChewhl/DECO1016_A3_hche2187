About Tokyo

Tokyo mixes the ultramodern and the traditional, from neon-lit skyscrapers to historic temples. It is also famous for its sakura tree-lined streets, shrines and temples, cuisine, and its excellent transport system

Where is Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital of Japan. It is located apporximately in the middle of Japan. To be more precise, it is located at the head of Tokyo Bay on the Pacific coast of central Honshu

How is Tokyo

Tokyo has a humid, subtropical climate with warm summers and cool winters, which sometimes can be very cold. Besides, the warmest month is August, and the coldest month is January

Why visit Tokyo

Tokyo offers a seemingly unlimited choice of shopping, entertainment, culture and dining to its visitors. The city's history can be appreciated in districts such as historic temples and gardens
